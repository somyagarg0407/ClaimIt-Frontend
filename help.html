❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often

🪪
Is Aadhaar card mandatory for all schemes?
▼
Yes, Aadhaar is mandatory for most government schemes as it ensures direct benefit transfer (DBT) to your linked bank account and prevents duplication. Some state-level schemes may accept alternative ID proof, but Aadhaar is always recommended.
💰
How long does it take to receive disbursement?
▼
Processing times vary by scheme. Central government schemes like PMAY typically take 30–90 days after approval. Scholarship disbursements usually happen quarterly. You can track your disbursement date in the My Claims section.
📋
Can I apply for multiple schemes at the same time?
▼
Yes! You can apply for multiple non-overlapping schemes simultaneously. However, you cannot claim two schemes that provide the same type of benefit (e.g., two housing subsidies). ClaimIt automatically flags any conflicts before you submit.
🏠
I live in a rented house — can I still apply for PMAY?
▼
PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana) requires that neither you nor any family member owns a pucca house anywhere in India. Renters who meet income criteria are eligible to apply, as the scheme is designed to help you purchase or construct a new home.
🔄
My application was rejected — can I appeal?
▼
Yes. Most schemes allow you to file a grievance or appeal within 3–6 months of rejection. Open the rejected claim in My Claims, scroll to the expanded details, and click File Appeal. You can also contact the nodal ministry directly.
🔒
Is my personal data safe on ClaimIt?
▼
Absolutely. ClaimIt uses AES-256 encryption for all stored data and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are ISO 27001 certified and never sell or share your data with third parties. You can request data deletion anytime from your account settings.
🌾
Is PM Kisan Samman Nidhi available for tenant farmers?
▼
PM-KISAN is primarily for landholding farmers. Tenant farmers and sharecroppers are generally not eligible unless their names appear in official land records as cultivators. Some states have separate state-level schemes for tenant farmers — check your state filter on Discover.
📱
Can I use ClaimIt in Hindi or regional languages?
▼
Yes! ClaimIt currently supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, and Gujarati in addition to English. Go to Settings → Language to switch. Our helpline is also available in 12 regional languages.
